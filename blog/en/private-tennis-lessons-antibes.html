Antibes: a distinctive tennis territory

Antibes occupies a unique position on the French Riviera. The town blends a lively historic centre, the Cap d'Antibes peninsula with some of the Riviera's most sought-after villas, and Juan-les-Pins, a seaside district heavily frequented in summer by an international clientele.

Port Vauban, one of Europe's largest marinas, also attracts a transient population used to fast, discreet and flexible service — expectations that naturally carry over into demand for private sports coaching.

"In Antibes, the demand for private tennis rarely comes from a need to compete. It comes from people who want a quality hour of sport, without organisational hassle, during a stay or a weekend."

Areas covered around Antibes

I come directly to your villa, residence or hotel court — in Antibes as well as the surrounding towns: Cap d'Antibes, Juan-les-Pins, Golfe-Juan, Sophia Antipolis, Biot and Vallauris.

The proximity to Sophia Antipolis in particular makes it easy to organise sessions for families who split their week across several towns — a very common profile in this area.

Who is private coaching right for here?

  • Villa owners in Cap d'Antibes with a private court, looking for a reliable coach over the long term
  • Second-home owners present only part of the year, with occasional but recurring needs
  • Visitors passing through the port or staying in a hotel in Juan-les-Pins, looking for a sports activity with no commitment
  • Players of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced players wanting to work on a specific technical point

International residents and visitors

Antibes and the Cap are home to a significant proportion of English-speaking and Italian-speaking residents and visitors. Being able to follow a lesson in your own language directly changes the quality of the exchange — especially for technical corrections, where vocabulary precision matters as much as demonstration.

Lessons are available in French, English and Italian, with no registration file or commitment required.

How to book

A WhatsApp message is enough to organise the first session. I confirm the time slot within 24 hours and come directly with the necessary equipment — no organisation is required on your part beyond the court's availability.
